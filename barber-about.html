Our Story

Built on Passion. Perfected by Practice.

‚ú¶

BarberHaus opened its doors in 2008 with a single chair, one vision, and an obsession with craft. Founder Marco Rossi returned from three years training under some of Italy's finest barbers and brought a philosophy back with him: every man who sits in that chair deserves the best twenty minutes of his week.

Today, BarberHaus has grown to five barbers across two locations, but the philosophy hasn't changed. We still sharpen our blades by hand, heat our towels fresh for every client, and refuse to rush a single cut.
